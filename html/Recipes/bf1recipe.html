Cheese Sandwich

Main Ingredient - Bread, Mozzarella cheese

⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ Rating

17 Mins
Easy to Cook

Description :

This Italian-style cheese sandwich is a delicious and filling meal that's perfect for lunch or a light dinner. It's made with two types of cheese, provolone and mozzarella, as well as fresh tomato and basil. The sandwich is lightly grilled in olive oil to give it a crispy exterior and melted cheese interior. The sourdough bread adds a tangy and slightly crunchy texture to the sandwich.

Ingredients :

  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2 slices of provolone cheese
  • 2 slices of mozzarella cheese
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 2-3 leaves of fresh basil
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
