Bread Omelette

Main Ingredient - Bread, Eggs, Vegetables

⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ Rating

Description :

Bread Omelette is a classic breakfast dish that combines the goodness of eggs with the deliciousness of bread. It's a quick and easy recipe that can be customized with your favorite vegetables and spices.

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 eggse

  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
