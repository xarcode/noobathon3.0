Slide - 01

  1. Toast a slice of whole grain bread until golden brown.

Time : 2:00 mins

Slide - 02

  1. Cut a ripe avocado in half and remove the pit.
  2. Scoop out the flesh and place it in a small bowl.

Time : 2:00 mins

Slide -03

  1. Mash the avocado with a fork until it reaches your desired consistency.

Time : 1:00 min

Slide - 04

  1. Add 1 tsp of olive oil and 1/4 tsp of salt to the mashed avocado. Mix well.

Time : 1:00 mins

Slide - 05

  1. Spread the mashed avocado on top of the toasted bread.

Time : 1:00 mins

Slide - 06

  1. Add optional toppings like sliced tomato, crumbled feta cheese, or a poached egg.
  2. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Time : 2:00 mins
